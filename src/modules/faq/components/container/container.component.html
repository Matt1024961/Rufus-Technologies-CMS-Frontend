Frequently Asked Questions

What is Wikipedia? Further information: Wikipedia:About Wikipedia is an encyclopedia which is free to use and edit. It is available in many different languages and on many devices. The content of Wikipedia is free to reproduce under the Creative Commons Attribution-Sharealike 3.0 Unported License (CC-BY-SA) and the GNU Free Documentation License (GFDL), except for some images. Who writes Wikipedia? Further information: Wikipedia:Administration Wikipedia is written and edited by volunteers from around the world. If you wish to help, you can start by visiting the community portal or the project pages. Is Wikipedia censored? Further information: Help:Censorship Wikipedia uses the minimum of censorship. Generally, only gratuitous explicit (pornographic) images are removed. The community of Wikipedia editors determine what text and images are displayed, so there may be material that offends some people. There may be those who are offended, shocked or disgusted by medical images, nudity, religious imagery and controversial images. How do I search Wikipedia?
